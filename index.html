Overview

World War II (1939–1945) was one of the largest and deadliest conflicts in history. It involved many countries around the world and changed global politics forever.

The war mainly involved the Allied Powers and Axis Powers, with battles fought in Europe, Africa, Asia, and the Pacific Ocean.

Important Timeline

1939 – Germany invades Poland.

1941 – Pearl Harbor attack.

1942 – Battle of Stalingrad.

1944 – D-Day invasion.

1945 – Germany and Japan surrender.
